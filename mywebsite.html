About Me

I'm a [junior] full-stack web developer based in Montreal. Creating is my passion: I'm trained as an artist (BFA 2019) and art historian (MA 2022). While I love painting and drawing, I will never forget the excitement of printing Hello World on a webpage. There is something surreal about publishing your [seemingly dull] creation on the WWW.
If I'm not coding, I'm either making food, eating food, running, or painting :D
